## 📋 3-Minute Extended Pitch (For Q&A and technical judges)

### Slide 1 / Pitch Open: The Problem (40 seconds)
"Good evening judges. Uttar Pradesh's Jansunwai Portal handles immense volume, but suffers from two massive gaps:
1. **The Translation Bottleneck**: Citizens submit in diverse vernacular formats like Hindi, Awadhi, or Hinglish. Automated filters break, requiring manual sorting.
2. **Operational Opacity**: Citizens have no idea which officer is working on their ticket or when it will be fixed.

We created **SunwAI**, which addresses this with 5 autonomous, cooperating AI agents."

### Live Demo Phase: The Citizen Journey (80 seconds)
"Let's look at the live portal.
Our citizen has a major issue. In Aliganj, a transformer is sparking. They write this in frantic Hinglish. 

When they submit, instead of passing it to a generic chatbot, **SunwAI** orchestrates a pipeline of specialized agents:
- **Agent 1 (Intake)** normalizes the raw text and flags the dialect as Hinglish.
- **Agent 2 (Classification)** performs semantic emotional analysis. It detects the keyword 'sparking' and 'fire hazard', marking the urgency as **Critical** and priority as **5/5**.
- **Agent 3 (Routing)** reads the live workload of all Lucknow departments. Seeing that LESA currently has 9 open tickets, it flags a potential overload but routes to node officer Er. Vinay Dwivedi.
- **Agent 4 (Communication)** drafts a warm, personalized SMS notification natively in Hindi, including the specific officer's name and a dynamically shortened SLA of 4 hours."

### The Administration Panel: Officer & Follow-up Agent (40 seconds)
"Now, let's switch to the **Officer Command Center**. 
Here, commissioners have an birds-eye view of all complaints in Lucknow Nagar Nigam. Note the critical banner warning at the top—LESA is currently approaching maximum capacity.

Because LESA is overloaded, if we run our **Autonomous Follow-up Agent (Agent 5)** loop, the agent automatically detects that the sparking transformer ticket has been delayed and escalates it to the Special Municipal Commissioner, raising priority and alerting supervisors.

Once an officer resolves the problem, a single click sends a final validation and success SMS to the citizen."

### Analytics & Engineering Focus: Closing (20 seconds)
"In the **Analytics Panel**, administrators get real-time graphs showing exactly which wards are overloaded. SunwAI is built for production, featuring offline keyword-fallback systems, PII protections, and standard API limits. 

We bring genuine agentic governance to Lucknow. Thank you, and we are open to questions!"
